📔 Class Diary — Home-school communication, simplified

A clear, no-ambiguity diary for daily classwork, homework, behaviour, and parent acknowledgement. Each entry is attached to a specific date, class, subject and (optionally) a period. Parents see only their own children's classes; teachers see only the classes they own.

How to use it: pick a date and class → fill the four quick sections (Classwork, Homework, Behaviour, Note) → hit Post diary. Parents can mark each entry as seen and leave an acknowledgement note.

Affordable one-time setup, no monthly bills: the entire platform runs on free Supabase + free hosting, and every feature is deterministic and rules-based (no AI API). We deliberately avoid monthly, termly or yearly subscriptions because many small schools cannot afford recurring fees — so the platform is offered as a single, low one-time setup fee. Your school owns its data forever and pays nothing to run it.
