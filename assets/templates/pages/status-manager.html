Role & Status Manager

👤Guestnot signed in
ℹ️ What is this page? Role & Status Manager (admin only). Safely change a person's platform role/status — for example promote a teacher to admin, convert a parent record to staff, or suspend an account — with a full audit trail.
▼

Who uses it: Super Admin / Admin only.

How to use it:

  1. Open Role & Status Manager.
  2. Search the person by name, email or admission/staff number.
  3. Pick the new role/status and confirm.
  4. The change applies immediately and is logged in the Activity Log.

Tip: click the ℹ️ Help button in the top bar, or the 💬 assistant, for the full explanation of any page.
