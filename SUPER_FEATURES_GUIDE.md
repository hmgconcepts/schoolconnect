# Super Features Guide — School Connect Gen v8

`assets/js/super.js` provides the shared "super" toolkit on every generated site:

1. **Chatbot / site assistant** — rules-based per-page help (`PAGE_HELP`, `explainPage`), 25+ topics, quick-reply chips.
2. **Command palette** — quick navigation across modules.
3. **ID cards** — student & staff templates (`vertical`, `corporate`), colours, photos (Google Drive links), QR codes, bulk **Print ALL**.
4. **Certificates** — designer with colours/fonts/layout/signature (`certificate_designs` table).
5. **Flyers** — templates (`poster`, `elegant`, `sidebar`), one-click palettes (`PALETTES`), sizes, ribbons, badges.
6. **Notifications helpers** — free channels (mailto:, wa.me, sms:).
7. **Data export/import + draft autosave** for school projects.
